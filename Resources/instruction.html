How to Read (in this experiment)

In this experiment, you'll be reading English sentences one word at a time.

At the start of each trial, you will see a series of dashes. To present the sentence, press the space bar to reveal each word one at a time. Try to read at a natural pace and don't rush. This will seem a bit confusing at first, but you should get familiar with the task fairly quickly.
